Rules

Spell Rules

1. Unforgivable spells are only to be used by the Dark lord and his deatheaters.
2. Say your spells out loud to avoid confusion.
3. You can not apperate (This counts for deatheaters and Pheonix members aswell).
4. Earth bending spells aren't allowed. (Like Aquamenti or incendio)

Acting Rules

1.  If you are under the influence off a spell/charm act like it.
2. Be carefull with other people's stuff.
3. Do not cause harm to other people and yourself.
4. Talk English (during the game).
5. It's a game, not a fight!
6. Don't fuck around/don't be stupid.
